My sajek tour during winter session

Author name: Abdulla Mutasaddik

Sajek Valley has its beauty to reveal in all seasons. Every season brings out a new colour of the nature whose beauty is incomparable. During winter days you can enjoy the chilly weather with a magical view but if you want to enjoy between the dancing clouds then rainy season is the perfect time for you. During rainy seasons you can enjoy the water falls as well as during summer they are mostly dry.

Attractive places in Sajek

Author name :Abdulla Mutasaddik

The dreamy view is one of the main attraction in Sajek. The beautiful rows of mountains and valleys hidden under the bed of clouds will win your heart. This wonderful place has three type of nature. It gets really hot at times during the day but cools down as the sun sets. During rainy season the place has its own beauty, it rains in one mountain and the other shines with a rainbow. It is truly a magical experience.

Rangamati hill district

Author:Abdulla Mutasaddik

Sajek is a union located in the north of Chittagong Hill Tracts. It's under Baghaichori Upazila in Rangamati hill district, it is situated 67 kilometres (42 mi) north-east from Khagrachhari town and 95 kilometres (59 mi) north from Rangamati city.
